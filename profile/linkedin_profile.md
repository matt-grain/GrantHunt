products—it's fundamentally transforming how we build them.


In 2025, we made a bold bet. We refactored our entire platform using
Claude and restructured our engineering team from 6 developers
to 2. The results? Deployment cycles dropped from 2 months to 2
weeks. Bug resolution time went from 3 weeks to days. We didn't just
cut costs—we built better, faster, and leaner.

the architecture behind it. After 25 years designing systems—from
embedded IoT to enterprise cloud, from M2M platforms to analytics
pipelines—I understand why things break and how to build them
right. That experience is what turns AI from a code generator into
a true force multiplier. Without architectural discipline, AI creates
technical debt at machine speed. With it, you get production-ready
systems that scale.

Application Architect and Data Scientist, and over two decades
architecting systems across IoT, M2M, cloud, embedded, and
enterprise—from Schlumberger to Orange to Atos, spanning Oil &
